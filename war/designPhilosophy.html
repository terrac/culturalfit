Culture Fit Philosophy

Culture fit tries to solve a specific problem. That problem is, how do I explain to someone that you are good at what you do if they don't know how to evaluate that?

The answer is that you don't. You look to a respected third party that can give you good data, in this case Culture Fit

Culture Fit should provide enough information to decide if someone is worth interviewing. Its goal is to say assuming this person is honest then there is a pretty good chance they know what they are doing.

Additionally there are also questions about different hobbies so you can focus on people that are a good team fit.
